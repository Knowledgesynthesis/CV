Web Applications & Tools

A collection of interactive tools and applications I developed to support clinical decision-making, medical education, and research methodology.

HCC Recurrence/Metastasis Risk Prediction Tool

Predicts the risk of recurrence or metastasis in patients with hepatocellular carcinoma using validated histologic and clinical predictors.

HCC Overall Survival Probability Calculator

Estimates overall survival probabilities for hepatocellular carcinoma patients, integrating tumor and patient-level data.

HCC Any Event Probability Calculator

Calculates disease-free survival and the likelihood of any adverse clinical event in hepatocellular carcinoma.

Statistical Test Selector

Guides users through a series of questions to determine the most appropriate statistical test for their dataset.

Measures of Association Calculator

Computes common epidemiological measures such as relative risk, odds ratio, and attributable risk.

Diagnostic Test Performance Calculator

Takes total patient count, disease prevalence, sensitivity, and specificity to generate a 2x2 table and calculate all diagnostic test measures.

Diagnostic Test Performance Analyzer

Takes a 2x2 table (true positives, true negatives, false positives, false negatives) and calculates all diagnostic test measures.

Potassium (K⁺) Interactive Spectrum

Interactive spectrum for hypo-, normo-, and hyperkalemia. Users can adjust K⁺ levels to see mnemonics and clinical correlations.

Maneuvers → Preload/Afterload → Murmurs

A dynamic visual and auditory simulator of cardiac murmurs. Demonstrates how bedside maneuvers affect preload, afterload, and murmur characteristics.

StatCalc

A lightweight statistical calculator for quick computation of common statistical measures.

Combine Mean & SD

Merges group means and standard deviations with visual illustrations of resulting distributions.

Board Review Questions

Interactive platform for practicing board-style review questions in medicine and research methodology.

Text Memorization App

A customizable text-based memorization tool to aid in efficient study and recall.

Biostatistics Review Questions

Interactive quiz app designed to reinforce key biostatistics concepts through practice questions.

Confounding vs Effect Modification Lab

Interactive lab demonstrating the differences between confounding and effect modification in epidemiologic studies.

Statistics & Epidemiology Quiz

A self-assessment tool covering core principles in statistics and epidemiology.
